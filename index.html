Our History

Integrity Academy was established in 2010, with academic activities officially commencing on September 13 of the same year. The school began with Daycare, Nursery, and Primary sections, and was founded by Engineer Olufemi Samuel. The first Headmistress was Madam Maria.

Our institution has grown from a small educational center to a comprehensive academy serving students from nursery through secondary levels, consistently maintaining high standards of education and discipline.

Situated Off Buachi Road, Behind Grace Baptist Church Tumpure, Gombe State, our academy provides a conducive learning environment with modern facilities, well-equipped classrooms, and dedicated spaces for various academic and extracurricular activities.

Daily Life at Integrity Academy

Every day at Integrity Academy is filled with opportunities for growth, discovery, and achievement. Our students begin their day with morning assembly, where they learn about values, current events, and celebrate achievements.

Throughout the day, students engage in interactive learning experiences, participate in group activities, and develop essential life skills. Our teachers create engaging lesson plans that make learning fun and meaningful.
