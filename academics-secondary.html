Secondary Department

Our Secondary section prepares students aged 12-18 years for academic excellence, national examinations, and future career success. We offer a rigorous curriculum combined with character development and leadership training.

Academic Programs

Core Subjects

  • • English Language & Literature
  • • Mathematics
  • • Sciences (Biology, Chemistry, Physics)
  • • Social Studies & History
  • • Computer Studies

Elective Options

  • • French Language
  • • Business Studies
  • • Fine Arts & Music
  • • Agricultural Science
  • • Technical Drawing

Examination Preparation

We prepare students for national examinations including WAEC, NECO, and JAMB. Our experienced teachers provide comprehensive exam preparation, practice tests, and revision programs to ensure success.

Leadership & Character

Beyond academics, we focus on developing leadership skills, ethical values, and responsible citizenship. Students participate in mentorship programs, community service, and leadership opportunities.

Career Guidance

Our career counseling services help students identify their strengths, explore career options, and make informed decisions about their future education and career paths.

Comprehensive library with resources for research and self-study.
