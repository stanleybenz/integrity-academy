Primary Department

Our Primary section provides comprehensive education for children aged 6-11 years, building strong foundations in core subjects while fostering critical thinking, creativity, and character development.

Core Subjects

English Language

Reading, writing, grammar, and communication skills development.

Mathematics

Number operations, problem-solving, and logical thinking.

Sciences

Basic Science, exploring the natural world through experiments.

Social Studies

History, geography, and civic education.

Languages

French and other language options for cultural enrichment.

Creative Arts

Art, music, and drama for creative expression.

Extracurricular Activities

We offer a variety of clubs and activities including sports, debate, choir, coding club, and more to help students discover their passions and develop leadership skills.

Assessment & Progress

We use continuous assessment methods to track student progress and provide regular feedback to parents. Our goal is to ensure every child reaches their full potential.

Learning Environment

Reading Corner

Encouraging reading habits from an early age.

Modern Classrooms

Well-equipped classrooms for interactive learning.

Sports & Recreation

Physical education and recreational activities.
