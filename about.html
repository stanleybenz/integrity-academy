Manager's Welcome

A Message from Mrs. Adekanmbi F.T

Dear Parents, Students, and Community Members,

Welcome to Integrity International Academy Gombe, where we are committed to nurturing the minds and character of our future leaders. Our academy stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Gombe State, Nigeria.

At Integrity Academy, we believe that education is not just about academic achievement, but about developing well-rounded individuals who are prepared to face the challenges of tomorrow with confidence, integrity, and leadership skills.

Our dedicated team of educators works tirelessly to create an environment where every student can thrive, discover their potential, and develop a lifelong love for learning.

We invite you to join our academic family and experience the difference that quality education makes in shaping the leaders of tomorrow.

Mrs. Adekanmbi F.T

School Manager

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops the intellectual, moral, social, and physical capabilities of every child, preparing them to become responsible citizens and leaders in a global society.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution in Gombe State and Nigeria, recognized for academic excellence, character development, and holistic education that transforms lives and communities.

Our Values

Honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our endeavors.

Excellence

Innovation

Embracing modern teaching methods and technology.

Our History

Integrity International Academy was established in 2010, with academic activities officially commencing on September 13 of the same year. The school began with Daycare, Nursery, and Primary sections, and was founded by Engineer Olufemi Samuel. The first Headmistress was Madam Maria An.

Our institution has grown from a small educational center to a comprehensive academy serving students from nursery through secondary levels, consistently maintaining high standards of education and discipline.
