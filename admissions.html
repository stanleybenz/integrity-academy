Admissions

Admission Process

  1. Submit a completed application form with required documents
  2. Attend an entrance assessment and interview (where applicable)
  3. Receive admission decision notification
  4. Complete registration and payment procedures
  5. Attend orientation and begin your journey with us

Required Documents

  • Birth certificate or declaration of age
  • Previous school records (transfer students)
  • Passport photographs
  • Medical records and immunization card
  • Parent/Guardian identification

Academic Levels

Nursery

Ages 2-5 years. Early childhood foundation program.

Primary

Ages 6-11 years. Comprehensive primary education.

Secondary

Ages 12-18 years. Advanced secondary curriculum.
