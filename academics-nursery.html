Nursery Department

Our Nursery section provides a nurturing and stimulating environment for children aged 2-5 years. We focus on early childhood development through play-based learning, fostering curiosity, creativity, and social skills.

Curriculum Highlights

Early Literacy

Introduction to letters, sounds, and early reading skills through interactive activities and stories.

Numeracy Skills

Basic counting, shapes, patterns, and number recognition through hands-on learning.

Creative Arts

Art, music, dance, and creative expression to develop imagination and fine motor skills.

Social Development

Building communication skills, empathy, and teamwork through collaborative activities.

Learning Approach

Our play-based curriculum encourages children to explore, discover, and learn at their own pace. We provide a safe and supportive environment where children can develop confidence, independence, and a love for learning.
